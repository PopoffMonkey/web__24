Spooky Story"

Akshay Suresh

Before they could react, the house plunged into darkness, and the whispers grew louder, closer, almost as if Eleanor was speaking just behind them.

They rushed to the door, but it would not budge, as though the house itself had trapped them.

Shadows lengthened around them, and in the dim glow of a flickering flashlight, Leo saw Eleanor standing in the hallway, her face pale and eyes like endless black wells.

Her mouth stretched wide as she leaned close, whispering, “Stay with me.”

The townsfolk who went searching the next morning found the door slightly ajar.

Inside, the house was silent, but a new portrait hung where Eleanors once had been. This one showed a group of teenagers, eyes wide with terror, frozen in their final moments—a permanent part of the house is haunted legacy.
